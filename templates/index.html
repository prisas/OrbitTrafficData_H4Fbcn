What is it useful for?

Here are some of the advantatges of usig Orbit Traffic Data but possibilites are unlimited.

Real-Time Traffic

Orbit offers a tool to visualize the traffic flow. It's top-notch technology.


Traffic Parameters

Orbit provides traffic parameters like rush hours traffic and crowds.


Chronological Stadistics

Efficient roads and public transport design with the stadistics provided.

Mobility Profiles

Each road will have a different mobility profile depending on it's flow.

Traffic Monitoring

Specific design to monitor the logistic vehicles of an enterprise or organization.

Timed Routes

Calculation and analysis of the vehicles routes and it's routes' time.
